Learn About The Apostasy

What is the apostasy?

The Apostasy (greek: apostasia) was a rebellion among the early church that the Savior and His apostles established.

Biblical Prophecies of Apostasy

The Apostles and Christ Himself prophesied of this Apostasy. Find references to these Biblical prophecies here.

Teachings of Early Christians

A focus of the teachings of early Christian leaders was to prevent corruption of doctrine. This was a main theme of the teachings of the apostles as well.
